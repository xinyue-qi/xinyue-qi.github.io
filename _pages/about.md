[Dr. Shouhao Zhou](https://sites.psu.edu/szhou/) in the Department of Public Health Sciences at Penn State College of Medicine  and [Dr. Christine B. Peterson](https://odin.mdacc.tmc.edu/~cbpeterson/) in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center.
